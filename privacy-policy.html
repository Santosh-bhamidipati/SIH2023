Game Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to our game ("the Game"). This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you, as a parent or guardian of a child aged 8-16, about how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of your child while they play our Game.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from your child:

  • Personal information such as name and age for account creation.
  • Game-related data, including in-game achievements and progress.
  • Technical information such as device type and operating system for gameplay analysis and optimization.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the Game experience.
  • To analyze gameplay patterns and make enhancements to the Game.
  • To communicate with parents or guardians regarding their child's gameplay or account.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your child's personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share information with service providers who assist us in operating the Game and maintaining its functionality.

Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review the personal information we have collected about your child.
  • Request changes or deletion of your child's information.
  • Withdraw your consent for your child to use the Game and have their data collected.
